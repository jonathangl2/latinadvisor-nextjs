Study and Work in Australia

Your Future Starts Here

Australia is one of the most popular destinations for international students seeking high-quality education, real job opportunities, and pathways to permanent migration. With vibrant coastal cities, a stable economy, and breathtaking natural landscapes, living in Australia is much more than an adventure — it’s an investment in your future.

5 Key Benefits of Studying in Australia

1. Legal Work Opportunities

With a student visa, you can work up to 24 hours per week while studying and full-time during holidays. This allows you to cover basic expenses, pay part of your tuition, and save during your stay. Australia has strong demand in industries such as hospitality, culinary arts, childcare and aged care, IT, engineering, construction, education, and more.

2. Stay After Graduation

After completing your program, you may apply for a Postgraduate Visa or extend your stay if you secure an employer willing to sponsor you. There are real pathways to remain in Australia legally, and our registered migration agents will guide you every step of the way.

3. High-Quality Education

Australia is home to internationally recognized English institutions and academic programs focused on practical and professional development, opening doors to global opportunities.

4. Exceptional Quality of Life

Australia consistently ranks among the countries with the highest quality of life. Its healthcare system, safety, transportation, and stunning natural environments make it an ideal place to live and study.

5. Travel with Your Partner or Family

Some programs allow your partner to obtain a visa with work rights and even include your school-aged children.

Wide Range of Academic Programs in Australia

Australia offers a diverse selection of high-quality academic programs tailored to your interests, goals, and English proficiency level:

English Courses:

  • General English
  • Business English
  • IELTS, Cambridge, and PTE Exam Preparation
  • English for Academic Purposes (EAP)

Vocational Education and Training (VET):

  • Certificates and Diplomas

Higher Education:

  • Bachelor’s Degrees
  • Master’s Degrees

Note: An advanced, certified level of English is required for higher education programs.

Essential Requirements for Your Australian Student Visa

The Australian government continuously improves its visa process, focusing on applicants who demonstrate a genuine intention to study. Factors such as your age, academic and professional background, financial capacity, and the quality of the educational institution you choose will be assessed.

Requirements Checklist:

1. CaConfirmation of Enrollment (CoE):

You must be enrolled in a government-approved course (CRICOS) and receive your official Confirmation of Enrollment. This document is mandatory for your visa application.

2. Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC):

Provides medical assistance with coverage based on the plan you select.

3. Statement of Purpose (GTE):

Allows you to demonstrate your genuine intention to stay in Australia temporarily as a student.

4. Valid Passport:

Must be valid for at least 6 months.

5. Medical Examination:

Depending on your country of origin and intended length of stay, the government may require medical exams before granting the visa.

6. Official Translations:

All documents must be translated into English.

7. Proof of Financial Capacity:

Depending on your nationality, you may need to demonstrate funds beyond tuition to support your stay in Australia. These funds only need to be available until your visa is approved — they are not an actual expense you must pay.

How much money do you need to show?

Amounts vary based on the duration of your program:

  • 3 months: approx. AUD $14,400
  • 6 months: approx. AUD $25,600
How can you prove it?

✅ Bank statements (last 3 months), or
✅ A family sponsor who supports your expenses.
