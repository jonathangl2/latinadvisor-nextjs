Migration consultations at LatinAdvisor

At LatinAdvisor, our registered migration agents Juan Bedoya (MARN 2117696), Renatto Lopez (MARN 2518857), and Christina Toftegaard (MARN 2117707) provide professional, transparent, and strategic advice designed to help you make informed and secure decisions about your future in Australia.

Your migration agent already knows who you are. We review your profile before the consultation. We do not start from zero.
No time limits. Your case is not measured by the clock. If more time is required, we take it. (Our record: 2 hours and 42 minutes.)
You leave with a clear strategy. You do not leave with unanswered questions. You leave with a migration pathway aligned with your real goals.
You receive your Letter of Advice. A formal document outlining the analysis, strategy, and recommended next steps. A guide you can refer to whenever you need it.
